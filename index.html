Oz Foundation!

About Us

Oz Foundation has been helping scholars since 2004. We are a family foundation that strongly believes a college education is the gateway to a successful future. We feel that financial hardship should not deter individuals from reaching this goal.

Oz Foundation operates in Santa Clara and San Diego Counties, supporting students who would otherwise not afford college. In addition to financial support, we provide mentorship to help students in their studies and adjustment to college life.

Prospective Scholars

Criteria:

  • You must be a high school senior or junior college transfer student.
  • You must have attended high school in Santa Clara County or San Diego County.
  • This program is for low-income families. Supporting documentation like tax returns is required.
  • You must have been accepted to a four-year accredited university.

All applicants will be notified by mail or telephone regarding acceptance or decline. Award amounts are based on individual need.

What to Expect if Accepted

Maintain your grades and enrollment as a full-time student (with supporting paperwork), and you will be part of our program for up to four years. You will also be assigned a mentor to communicate directly with. We generally accept 2-5 new students per year.
